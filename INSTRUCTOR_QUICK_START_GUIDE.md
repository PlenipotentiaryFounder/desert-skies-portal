## ğŸ“– Your Instructor Logbook

### Overview
Your logbook is **automatically updated** when you complete a mission debrief. No manual entry needed!

### What You'll See
- **Totals Dashboard** at top:
  - Total Time, PIC, Dual Given
  - Cross Country, Night, Instrument
- **Logbook Entries Table**:
  - Date, Aircraft, Flight Times
  - Status badges (Draft/Final/Voided)
  - Click any row for full details

### How It Works
1. You schedule a mission
2. Fly with your student
3. Complete the debrief (with optional AI analysis)
4. **System automatically creates TWO logbook entries**:
   - One for the student (dual received)
   - One for you (dual given, PIC)
5. Both entries are immediately visible
